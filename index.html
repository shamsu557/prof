Professor Shamsu Sabo

Professor Shamsu Sabo is a distinguished academic with expertise in computer science and engineering. With over 20 years of experience, he has contributed significantly to fields such as the Internet of Things (IoT), embedded systems, and automation technologies. His academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Embedded Systems, and his publications have influenced researchers worldwide. He has also authored several books and collaborated on numerous international projects.

His work in IoT applications for healthcare and smart home systems has garnered international recognition. As an educator, Professor Sabo has mentored hundreds of students and leads groundbreaking research initiatives.
